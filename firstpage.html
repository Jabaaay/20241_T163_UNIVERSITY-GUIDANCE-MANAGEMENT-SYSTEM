About Us

40 years of Sarap ng buhay sa tinapay

A growing need for bread products. A strong faith in God. A name started the Julie's Bakeshop on January 6, 1981, in Wireless, Mandaue City.

Mrs. Julia Gandionco fondly called Ms. Julie, was running three canteens when she ventured into the bakeshop business. From this innovative response to the ever-increasing need for bread in Ma'am Julie's canteen operations, the country largest bakeshop chian was, Julie's Bakeshop.
